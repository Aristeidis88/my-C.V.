Few things about me

Having acquired ten years of experience in Audiovisual events and performing arts, I have worked for six years in concerts and live events as a Technician, Sound Designer, F.O.H. and Monitor Engineer. And five as audiovisual Technician in conference and seminar events. Some of my personality traits are patience, the need to improve my qualifications and team spirit. I was born at Athens in Greece, went to primary and high school for electritians at Aigio City. At the the age of 17 I started studie as Sound Enginner at IEK AKMI college in Athens. After two years I took my Licence with A and started work for Exhibit Audiovisual Company. Gained experience for 5 years working for Conference and Events. In 2012 I complete my army dutys to Greek army. In 2013 I started work for PM sound and lights company where I was Technitian manager, warehouse manager and system Technitian for Lacoustics Technologie. in 2019 I moved at Glasgow and since then I have done hundreds events as self-employment Sound Enginner. At Octomber of 2020 I started studying in IT professional training school for Web Application Development course.
